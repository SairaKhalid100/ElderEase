The Problem We Solve

Many elderly people need help with everyday tasks such as medicine management, doctor appointments, transportation, grocery shopping, technology support, form filling and companionship. Families often worry about loved ones but cannot always be physically present. ElderEase bridges this gap through trusted and verified support.

Our Story

Hi, I'm Saira Khalid, a Grade 7 student of The Shri Ram School, Moulsari, Gurugram and the founder of ElderEase. I created ElderEase after recognizing that many seniors need assistance with daily tasks but do not require full-time care. Our goal is to help seniors remain independent while providing families with peace of mind.

How It Works

1. Request Support

Families tell us what support their loved one needs.

2. Verified Matching

We connect seniors with trained and verified helpers.

3. Service Delivery

Services are delivered safely and reliably.

4. Peace of Mind

Families stay informed while seniors receive trusted support.
